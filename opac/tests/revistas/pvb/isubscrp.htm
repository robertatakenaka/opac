SUBSCRIPTION

 

Keep yourself informed through subscription of Pesquisa Veterinária Brasileira, a Brazilian Journal of Veterinary Research, published with support of CNPq, CAPES, CFMV and in partnership with Embrapa.

The journal is edited monthly by the Brazilian College of Animal Pathology (CBPA), with collaboration of the Brazilian Association of Wildlife Medicine (Abravas), and the Brazilian College of Animal Anatomy (CBAA), publishing papers on Livestock Diseases, Nutrition and Reproduction, Small Animal Diseases, Wildlife Medicine, and Animal Morphophysiology.

Annual subscriptions from foreign countries should be made through Subscription Services, as there is Swets (www.swets.com) or Ebsco (E-mail: jtubbs@ebsco.com)

The online journal is free and open access.
