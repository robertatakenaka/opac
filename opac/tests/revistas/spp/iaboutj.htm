Basic Information

São Paulo em Perspectiva is a quarterly publication, aimed at reporting articles with conjectural and structural analyses of local or international economic, social and political phenomena in several areas. The texts published are written by researchers from Fundação Seade or other public and private organizations, universities and research centers.

The journal is differs from other publications for presenting works with both theoretical and technical characteristics, which are grouped, in each issue, according to the topic proposed and addressed in a detailed fashion. Therefore, São Paulo em Perspectiva publishes different concepts that are complementary or even opposite, enabling the problematization of distinct views of the topic selected.

The abbreviated title Sao Paulo Perspec. should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
