Published every three months by the Associação Brasileira de Cerâmica - ABCeram (Brazilian Ceramic Society), the journal Cerâmica is distributed to members of the Society and to libraries worldwide.

Subscription orders for the printed version of the journal should be addressed to the Principal Editor.

The online journal is free and open access.
