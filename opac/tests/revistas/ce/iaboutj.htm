Basic information

Cerâmica, the official journal of the Associação Brasileira de Cerâmica - ABCeram (Brazilian Ceramic Society), accepts original (and review) articles in the entire field of ceramic science and technology, including bioceramics, advanced ceramics, structural ceramics, electroelectronic ceramics, magnetic ceramics, optical ceramics, nuclear ceramics, ceramic raw materials, cements, sanitaryware and porcelain, thermal insulators, ceramic tiles, refractories, glazes and ceramic pigments, glasses and glass-ceramics, ceramic matrix composites, basic science, ceramic art, characterization techniques, ceramic equipments, microstructural analysis, manufacturing processes, powder synthesis, etc.

Cerâmica is published quarterly. All contributions are independently refereed. Printed copies are distributed to members of the Brazilian Ceramic Society and to libraries worldwide.

The Brazilian Ceramic Society was founded in 1953, and in March 1955 Cerâmica was first released. Its earliest issue published the papers presented at the first Brazilian Ceramic Congress, held in São Paulo in 1954.

Any correspondence to the journal should be addressed to the Editors.

The Brazilian Ceramic Society assumes no responsibility for the contents of the technical papers and for the statements and opinions advanced by the contributors.

The abbreviated title of the journal is Cerâmica, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
