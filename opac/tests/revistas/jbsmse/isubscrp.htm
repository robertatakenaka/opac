SUBSCRIPTION

 

The Journal of the Brazilian Society of Mechanical Sciences and Engineering is published quarterly and is distributed freely to members of the Brazilian Society of Mechanical sciences (ABCM). 

Annual institutional subscription rates for the printed version of the journal:

  • US$ 320.00 for institutions
  • US$ 120.00 to individuals

Issues are airmail shipped. All subscriptions are payable in advance and entered an annual basis.
