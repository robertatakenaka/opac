Basic Information

The on-line quarterly journal Sexualidad, Salud y Sociedad (Rio de Janeiro) is a project by the Latin American Center on Sexuality and Human Rights (CLAM-IMS/UERJ), seeking to promote the exchange of Latin American scholarly production and its dissemination among researchers, activists and policymakers. The journal will welcome unpublished articles which, focusing on the contexts of countries in the region, explore the cultural and political dimensions of sexuality.

Its abbreviated title is Sex., Salud Soc. (Rio J.), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
