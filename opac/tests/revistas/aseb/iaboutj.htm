Basic information

The Anais da SEB was created in 1972, by the Entomological Society of Brazil, with the purpose of publishing results of original research on the several specialties of entomology, like bionomics, systematics, morphology, physiology, behavior, ecology, biological control, crop protection and on acarology.

From the first issue (jan-march) of 2001 on, the title of the journal and the format change to Neotropical Entomology, although keeping the sequence of numbering.

The short title An. Soc. Entomol. Bras. Brasil is recommended to be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
