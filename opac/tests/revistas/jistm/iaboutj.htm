Basic information

JISTEM Journal of Information Systems and Technology Management.

JISTEM is a peer-reviewed free online international journal. The journal is published every four months. JISTEM was created in 2004 by Prof. Edson Luiz Riccio and is published by TECSI - FEA/USP

JISTEM mission is to publish significant research relevant to the field of Information Systems and Technology Management and Information Sciences in the organizations and society in a multidisciplinary perspective.

JISTEM is assessed by the Brazilian System Qualis/CAPES.

Its abbreviated title is JISTEM., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
