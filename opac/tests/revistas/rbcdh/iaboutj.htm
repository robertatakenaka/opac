Basic Information

The Revista Brasileira de Cineantropometria e Desempenho Humano (RBCDH) is an open access, peer reviewed journal, published by the "Centro de Desportos da Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina", Florianópolis, SC. Brazil.
The RBCDH has been publishing since 1999. The journal's publication periodicity was annual from 1999 (vol. 1) to 2002 (vol. 4), biannual from 2003 (vol. 5) to 2005 (vol. 7), quarterly from 2006 (vol. 8) to 2009 (vol. 11) and bimonthly since 2010 (vol. 12).

The mission of the journal is to disseminate original scientific research that contributes to the advancement of human movement sciences with emphasis on kinanthropometry in both its morphological and functional aspects as well as the conditioning factors of physical performance.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. bras. cineantropom. desempenho hum. (Online), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
