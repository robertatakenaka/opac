Getting the journal

The Memorias is published under the Open Access model and is therefore free for anybody to read and download, and to copy and disseminate for educational purposes.

Content is freely accessible to readers and no publication fees are charged to authors.

All content is published via a Creative Commons licence.

The journal is completely financed by institutional funding from the Instituto Oswaldo Cruz, Fiocruz and grants from public funding agencies.
