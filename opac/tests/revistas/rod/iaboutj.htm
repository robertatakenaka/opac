Basic Information

Rodriguesia – the scientific periodical of the Rio de Janeiro Botanical Garden was created in 1935 and since then has been published quarterly. The Journal publishes original scientific articles, reviews, opinions and scientific notes in various areas of plant biology (taxonomy, systematics and evolution, physiology, phytochemistry, ultrastructure, cytology, anatomy, palynology, development, genetics, reproductive biology, ecology, ethnobotany and phylogeography), as well as the History of Botany and topics related to Botanical Gardens.

Its abbreviated title is Rodriguésia, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
