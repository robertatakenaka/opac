Basic information

The aim of the Rio Grande do Sul Dental Journal (RGO) is to disseminate and promote the exchange of information in the various areas of dental research, providing the national and international scientific communities with a formal channel of communication and thereby contributing to the advancement of knowledge.

Created in 1953, the RGO is a quarterly publication. The quarterly volumes are issued in March, June, September and December.

The abbreviated title of the journal is RGO, Rev Gaúch. Odontol., a format which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographical captions.
