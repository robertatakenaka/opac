Basic information

Journal of Aerospace Technology and Management (JATM) is an open access journal, devoted to research and management on different aspects of aerospace technologies. The content has been published continuously since January 2018. Its abbreviated title is J. Aerosp. Technol. Manag.  which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips. All the published material is free of any Article Processing Charges (APC).
