Since 2008, current and past issues of Topoi. Revista de História are available online, free access, at http://www.revistatopoi.org.

Teaching and research institutions may order copies of back issues, from number 1 (July-Dec 2000) to 16 (Jan-June 2008), free of charge, while stocks last, by writing to topoi@revistatopoi.org.

The online journal is free and open access.
