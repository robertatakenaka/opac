Basic Information

Psicologia em Estudo has been published regularly three times a year since 1996 by the Departamento de Psicologia (Psychology Department) of Universidade Estadual de Maringá - (State University of Maringá-PR www.dpi.uem.br).

Its objective is to publish original works in the psychology field: 1. research reports, 2. theoretical studies, 3. reports on professional experience, 4. Book reviews, 5. debates, 6. Interviews.

To briefly refer to the journal, the short title Psicol. estud. is recommended regarding bibliographies, footnotes, as well as bibliographical strips and references.
