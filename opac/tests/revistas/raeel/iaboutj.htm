Basic information

RAE-eletrônica is a generalist Business Administration journal that tries to address a wide range of knowledge domains, perspectives and issues.

RAE-eletrônica was released in January, 2002, with an academic and scientific profile and intended for professors, researchers and students in the fields of Management and allied areas. One of the goals in releasing RAE-eletrônica was to acquire agility in the publication of original Management papers. Furthermore, the free and comprehensive access provided by the Internet lend increased visibility to RAE-eletrônica collaborators.

We point out that RAE-eletrônica was re-assessed by Qualis/CAPES in 2005 and rated as A/Nacional (A/Domestic grade). RAE-eletrônica was also assessed and approved by Cabell Publishing and included into the 10th Edition of Cabell's Directory of Publishing Opportunities in Management.

Its abbreviated title is RAE-eletrônica, and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
