Basic information

Revista da Associação Médica Brasileira intended to publish original scientific papers in medicine.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Assoc. Med. Bras., it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

 

Indexing Information

Articles published in the journal are indexed by:

  • SciELO
  • Science Citation Index Expanded (SCIE)
  • Web of Science, Institute for Scientific Information (ISI)
  • Index Copernicus
  • LILACS
  • MEDLINE
  • CAPES - QUALIS B3
  • Scopus
