Basic Information

Ciência e Agrotecnologia (Science and Agrotechnology) is a journal bimonthly edited by Editora UFLA, with the support of the Research and Graduation Prorectories of the Federal University of Lavras. It publishes scientific articles in fields of Vegetal and Animal Sciences, including Agronomy, Food Science and Technology, Agrobusiness Economy and Administration, Rural Engineering, Veterinary and Animal Science.

The abbreviation of its title is Ciênc. agrotec., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic legends.
