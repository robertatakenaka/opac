Basic information

Revista Brasileira de Psiquiatria (RBP) is the official publication of Associação Brasileira de Psiquiatria (ABP - Brazilian Psychiatric Association).

Former Revista ABP, RBP is published quarterly in March, June, September, and December. Its purpose is to publish original works from all areas of psychiatry, focusing on public health, clinical epidemiology, basic sciences, and mental health problems that are relevant in our setting.

Contents published represent the opinions of the authors and do not necessarily reflect the opinions of the Editorial Board.

The abbreviated title of the journal is Rev. Bras. Psiquiatr.
