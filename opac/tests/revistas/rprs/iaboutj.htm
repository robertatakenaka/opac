Basic Information

Revista de Psiquiatria do Rio Grande do Sul is the official scientific publication of Associação de Psiquiatria do Rio Grande do Sul (APRS, Brazil). 

The journal is published four-monthly and aims to publish current and original research covering the broad spectrum of clinical psychiatry and basic science, produced by expert national and international bodies.

The abbreviated title of the journal Rev. psiquiatr. Rio Gd. Sul, which should be used in bibliographies, bibliographical references and strips.

As of volume 33, issue no. 3, Revista de Psiquiatria do Rio Grande do Sul has changed its title to Trends in Psychiatry and Psychotherapy.

 

Information Services

The articles published in Revista de Psiquiatria do Rio Grande do Sul are indexed or summarized by:

  • ABEC – Associação Brasileira de Editores Científicos
  • BIOSIS Publisher of Biological Abstracts and Zoological Record
  • Embase
  • LILACS – Index Medicus Latino-Americano
  • PsycINFO – American Psychological Association
  • SciELO Brasil – Scientific Electronic Library Online
  • Scopus
