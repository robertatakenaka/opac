SUBSCRIPTION

 

Hoehnea does not have a subscription service. Printed issues can be purchased at the Publications Sales Section of the Institute of Botany (Instituto de Botânica) (+55-11-5067-6028) or on the website:

http://intranet.seade.gov.br/publishop/

The issues in digital format are available for download at:

http://www.ibot.sp.gov.br/publicacoes/hoehnea/sobre_hoehnea.php

Hoehnea is also distributed through regular exchange with 195 national and international research institutions.

The online journal is free and open access.
