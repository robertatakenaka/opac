Basic information

Clinics is edited monthly by Hospital das Clínicas da Faculdade de Medicina da Universidade de São Paulo (HC-FMUSP) and publishes peer-reviewed articles of interest to clinicians and researchers in the medical sciences.

Clinics should not be abbreviated in bibliographies, footnotes, and bibliographical references and strips.
