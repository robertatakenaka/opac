Basic Information

Full title of the journal: Letras de Hoje - Estudos e debates em linguística, literatura e língua portuguesa.

Short title: Let. Hoje

Objective: Letras de Hoje, a quarterly publication of the Postgraduate Program in Letters of PUCRS, has the mission of publishing and disseminating knowledge in Linguistics, Theory of Literature, Literature and Portuguese Language, aiming at the diffusion of the Literature area.

Areas of interest: Linguistics, Theory of Literature, Literature and Portuguese Language.

History: The Letras de Hoje, published by the Graduate Program in Letters of PUCRS, appeared in the year 1967. At the origin of this common project are the figures of Elvo Clemente, Plínio Cabral and Leonor Scliar Cabral. For some time, the magazine was sponsored by representatives of the gaucho society, among them, Paulo Vellinho, José Octavio Bertaso (Livraria do Globo), and also some numbers financed by CNPq. Due to its growth, with the acceptance and collaboration of scholars from Brazil and abroad, the Journal started to count on a specific budget in the University budget, in the management of the Rector Brother Norberto Francisco Rauch. Since then, it has been edited by EDIPUCRS.

 Periodicity of the journal: Quarterly publication.
