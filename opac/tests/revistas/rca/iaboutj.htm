Basic Information

The Revista Ciência Agronômica is a scientific journal (four number/year) edited by the Federal University of Ceará, in Ceará State, Brazil since 1971. The creation of the Journal was a natural result of the need to spread the knowledge acquired in the agrarian science by the researchers of the old Agronomy School. Nowadays, the Journal of Agronomical Science has among its readers professors, researchers and students of areas similar to agronomy from universities all over Brazil and foreign countries

It is addressed to professors, scientists, students and others interested in the fields of Agrarian Sciences, Animal Sciences, Fishing, Food Production and Natural Resources.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Ciênc. Agron., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
