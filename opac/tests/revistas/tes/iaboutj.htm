Basic Information

Trabalho, Educação e Saúde (Work, Education and Health) is a scientific journal published by Escola Politécnica de Saúde Joaquim Venâncio <http://www.epsjv.fiocruz.br>, Fundação Oswaldo Cruz <http://www.fiocruz.br>. The journal, published every four months, seeks to foster technical or applied discussions, analyses, and investigations related to professional education in health.

Professional education and qualification and work processes in health are central themes of the magazine. In this regard, the journal seeks to consolidate Education as an area of knowledge in the field of health, in addition to contribute to the qualification of specific educational practices in this field.

The journal's target audience consists of researchers, graduate and postgraduate students, professionals linked to health services, and those involved in teaching and administration in the field of professional education in health.

Its abbreviated title is Trab. educ. saúde (Online), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

The full content of the current and previous issues of Trabalho, Educação e Saúde  is available online to readers in open and free access, on the following platforms: journalhomepage, FIOCRUZ Journals Portal and SciELO.
