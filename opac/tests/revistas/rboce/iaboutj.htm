Basic Information

Title: Revista Brasileira de Oceanografia

Editor-in-Chief: Profa. Dra. Ana Maria Setubal Pires Vanin

Objective: A journal for the publication of original contributions on Oceanography and related sciences.

Historical: The first publication of the Oceanographic Institute in 1950, the Boletim do Instituto Paulista de Oceanografia, has been highlighted as a diffuser of original research in the field of oceanography. Published from 1950 to 1951, in 1952 was called Boletim do Instituto Oceanográfico until 1996.

From 1996 to 2003, aiming to broaden the dissemination of Revista Brasileira de Oceanografia and finally in 2004, Brazilian Journal of Oceanography for an internacional approach.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. bras.oceanogr., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
