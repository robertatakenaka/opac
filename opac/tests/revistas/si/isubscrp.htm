SUBSCRIPTION

 

The Welding and Inspection Journal is distributed free of charge in CD to members of the Brazilian Welding Society (Associação Brasileira de Soldagem, ABS). By request, it is also available, in printed version, to libraries of research and teaching facilities, and to companies related to the welding and inspection fields. If you need further information or wish to request an issue of Welding and Inspection, please contact the Brazilian Welding Society (R. Dr. Guilherme Bannitz, nº 126 conj 42, Itaim-Bibi, São Paulo, 04532-060, SP, Tel. (55 11)3045.5040, abs@abs‑soldagem.org.br).

Please notice that the electronic version of Welding and Inspection can be freely accessed in: http://abs-soldagem.org.br/s&i/

The online journal is free and open access.
