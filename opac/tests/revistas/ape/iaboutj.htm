Basic information

Founded in 1988 Acta Paulista de Enfermagem is the technical-scientific electronic publication of the Escola Paulista de Enfermagem (Paulista School of Nursing - EPE) of the Universidade Federal de São Paulo (Federal University of São Paulo - UNIFESP). ISSN (electronic version): 1982-0194. Its mission is to promote scientific knowledge generated in the rigor of the research methodology and ethics. The purpose of Acta is to publish the outcomes of original research to advance the practice of nursing in the medical, surgical management, education, research and information technology and communication. The issues are published bimonthly (Jan/Feb) (Mar/Apr) (May/Jun) (Jul/Aug) (Sept/Oct) (Nov/Dec), with supplementary publications possible. Acta follows the Code of Conduct and Best Practice Guidelines for Journal Editors of the http://publicationethics.org/.

The abbreviated title of Acta Paulista de Enfermagem is Acta Paul Enferm. which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references, citations, and bibliographic legends.
