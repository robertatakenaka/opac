SUBSCRIPTION

 

Ciência Rural is a scientific journal published by the Editorial board of the Rural Science Center, Federal University of Santa Maria, RS, Brazil. The papers published in the journal are reviewed by at least two person selected from a broad list of referees. Ciência Rural is an international journal devoted to all aspects of the Rural Science area. The major fields related to agronomy, animal science, forestry and veterinary medicine are accepted for consideration.
