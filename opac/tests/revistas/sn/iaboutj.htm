Basic Information

The Sociedade & Natureza journal is an biannual publication of Geography Institute and Pos-Graduation Program on Geography of Universidade Federal de Uberlândia (Brazil).

The main goals of the journal is publish papers with critical revision about Geography issues and thesis scientific connections or results of conceptual, experimental and empiric research, foment experience exchanges in its specialty with other national and international institutions, which sustain similar publications, respect and defend the pluralism principles about philosophical, politic and scientific ideas.

The Sociedade & Natureza journal was created in 1989 and in 2004 become to be published in an online version. Actually, professor Silvio Carlos Rodrigues is the Editor in Chief, which takes his Doctoral Degree in Physical Geography in 1998 at Universidade de São Paulo.

Its abbreviated title is Soc. nat. (Online), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
