Basic information

The Psicologia Clínica is editated twice a year to publish original papers in the area of Psicologia Clínica, contributing for the good quality of the scientific production and the consolidation of this field in Brazil. Areas of interest: Psichoanalysis: clinic and culture; Language and construction of the subjectivity; Family and couple: psyco-social studies in psycotherapy; Clinic and neuroscience.

Its abbreviated title is Psicol. Clin., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
