Basic Information

Complete title: Revista Ilha do Desterrro:  A Journal of English Language, Literatures in English and Cultural Studies.

General Editor: Anelise Reich Corseuil

Objectives and History:
The Journal Ilha do Desterro publishes, since 1979, unpublished articles, reviews interviews in the areas of English, Literature and Cultural Studies. Ilha do Desterro is a periodical of the Programa de Pós-Graduação em Letras:Inglês e Literatura Correspondente from Federal University of Santa Catarina, published once each semester. Its greatest mission is to spread academic research results in the ares of English, Literature and Cultural Studies.

Periodicity: Quarterly

Its abbreviated title is Ilha Desterro, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
