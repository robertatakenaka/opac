SUBSCRIPTION

 

The subscription can be made by:

1. Being member of ABORL-CCF (Associação Brasileira de Otorrinolaringologia e Cirurgia Cérvico-Facial), through payment of the annual fee. The affiliation must be made through the official site of ABORL-CCF at http://www.aborlccf.org.br/conteudo/default.asp

2. Asking for the subscription of the journal through the email revista@aborlccf.org.br

Reprints of articles may be request also through the aforementioned email.
