Basic Information

The Brazilian Journal Geology (BJG) is the main publication of the Brazilian Society of Geology (SBG), with 4 annual issues, and which has been edited since 1971, replacing the former SBG Bulletin, published since 1952. The publication has an editorial and advisory board consisting of a select group of specialists. It currently has an online and a printed version, distributed to the associated who have no debts concerning the annuity of the year of the publication and who wish to receive it in printed version, entities and Brazilian and foreign libraries, maintaining an Exchange regimen with several international institutions.

Its abbreviated title is Braz. J. Geol., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
