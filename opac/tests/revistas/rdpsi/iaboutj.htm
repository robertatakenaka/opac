Basic information

The objective of Revista do Departamento de Psicologia - UFF is to diffuse and discuss the academic and scientific production. Its purpose is to recognize the need of coexistence among the many kinds of research in the psychology field, stimulating the constant debate as a mean of encouraging the scientific production. At the same time, it aims to motivate the dialog with different fields of knowledge, whose subjects are related to the studies of subjectivity.

The Revista do Departamento de Psicologia - UFF was created in 1989 by the initiative of the teachers of the UFF Psychology Department, with the objective of creating a vehicle of propagation based on which the academic production, directly or indirectly related to the field of Psychology, could be reunited in a favorable space for debate. For that purpose, the journal was organized to reunite different academic works, not only different for its contents but also for the style of their construction. There are articles, reports or scientific communication and bibliographic abstracts that express the research and extension activities of the researchers. In the last years, the Journal has gone through some modifications on its graphic project and editorial management.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Dep. Psicol., UFF, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and and bibliographical references and strips.
