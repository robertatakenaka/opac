About the journal

Tempo Social to publish original contributions in Sociology; Sociological Theory; Other specific sociologies.

The online journal is published under the Open Access model.

Its abbreviated title is Tempo soc., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and and bibliographical references and strips.

Periodicity: triannual publication
