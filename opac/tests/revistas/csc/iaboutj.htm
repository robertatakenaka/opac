Basic information

Launched at the end of 1996 and edited by the Associação Brasileira de Saúde Coletiva/Abrasco, the Revista Ciência & Saúde Coletiva is a scientific space for discussions, debates, research presentations, exposure of new ideas and controversies in the area. As of 2011, the journal has been published monthly, and currently publishes 12 annual issues. Each issue contains 10 to 12 thematic articles and approximately 20 articles on varied topics, opinions and reviews, comprising a total of 30 articles. Currently the journal receives an average of 2300 original articles per year. In its history there has been a visible growth in the participation of national and international authors, and an increased number of articles published in collaboration with researchers from English, French and Spanish-speaking countries. Since its origin the Revista Ciência & Saúde Coletiva fully complies with the requirements for periodicity and standardization for scientific publication, according to the rules set forth at the Vancouver Convention, which are usually applied to the fields of medicine and public health. Since 2014 the journal publishes the printed version in Portuguese and publishes the online version, at the SciELO database, in English and Portuguese.
