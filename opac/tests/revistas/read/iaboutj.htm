Basic Information

Aims:

To use electronic media to disseminate scientific knowledge about Administration produced in Brazil and abroad in order to encourage academic debate and assist professionals working in the area with their activities.

Areas of interest:

REAd is open to all areas related to the management of organizations.

Background:

REAd  (Revista Eletrônica de Administração), established in 1995 and published by the Escola de Administração da Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul, was the first electronic journal in the field in Latin America. It currently has a collection of over 600 published manuscripts. The REAd currently is rated B3 at CAPES, is indexed in SciELO collection, becoming able to level B1 in the next reviews, and appears in many other indexes.

Frequency of the journal:

Every 4 months

Other pertinent data:

REAd’s entire collection is available at http://seer.ufrgs.br/read. REAd is a periodical publication, which from 2007 became a every 4 months open to all topics relating to management of organizations. Occasionally, REAd publishes special editions, which are thematic editions, published in English.

The abbreviated title of the journal that should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, and bibliographic references is REAd. Rev. eletrôn. adm. (Porto Alegre).
