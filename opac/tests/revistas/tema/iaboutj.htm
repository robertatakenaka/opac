Basic Information

The journal TEMA (São Carlos) was created in 1999 - as an initiative of the Brazilian Society of Computational and Applied Mathematics - SBMAC - with the main goal of promoting the work of Brazilian researchers that present innovative applications and / or bring significant contributions to Mathematics. Since 2006 the journal TEMA has been receiving also manuscripts independent of the proceedings, allowing to receive submission of articles from the academic public in general.

In 2010 the electronic submission based on the SEER/OJS (http://seer.ibict.br/index.php) system has been implemented. In 2011, SBMAC joined the CrossRef (http://www.crossref.org) database. All articles published by TEMA since 2000 (volume 1) got indexed and identified by a DOI. Finally in 2012 the TEMA journal got accepted by SciELO Brasil, consolidating his recognition by the academic community.

Its abbreviated title is TEMA (São Carlos), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
