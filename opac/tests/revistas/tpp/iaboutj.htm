Basic information

Tropical Plant Pathology - Official publication of the Brazilian Phytopathological Society, located in Brasília, Federal District, Brazil. The journal is published bimonthly.

Tropical Plant Pathlogy - Begins with vol. 33, issue 1, January-February 2008, following the numbering sequence of its predecessor, Fitopatologia Brasileira, which was published from 1976 to 2007, vol. 1 to vol. 32.

The journal's short title is Trop. Plant Pathol., which should be used in footnotes and bibliographical references.
