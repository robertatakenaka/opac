SUBSCRIPTION

 

The Journal of Seed Science is a publication addressed to members of ABRATES, and to members of the national and international scientific community who work in the field of Science and Seed Technology. The JSS will be freely distributed to members of ABRATES and also for education and research institutions within the country and abroad.

See more information about the Association and how to subscribe to JSS, as well as values and benefits, through the site www.abrates.org.br, or by contacting ABRATES through E-mail abrates@abrates.org.br, or by Phone/Fax: (43) 3025-5120
