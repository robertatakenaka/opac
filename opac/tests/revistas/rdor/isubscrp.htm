SUBSCRIPTION

 

The magazine Revista Dor - Pesquisa, clínica e terapêutica (Research, clinics and therapeutics), is sent to the members of the Brazilian Society for the Study of Pain (SBED), upon the payment of the annual membership fee and gratuitously to libraries, associations, universities, colleges, medical societies, SBED regionals and Pain Leagues registered on the SBED website. The on line journal has open and free access.
