Basic Information

Objectives - To publish original and review articles and case reports on acute or chronic pain pathophysiology and therapy study in humans and animals.

Fields of interest - Acute pain, Chronic pain, Ethics and pain, Pain in humans, Pain in animals.

History - Revista Dor: Pesquisa, Clínica e Terapêutica was founded in 1999 by the Brazilian Society for the Study of Pain (SBED), with the primary objective of being a body to spread original and review articles and case reports on acute and chronic pain pathophysiology and therapy study in humans and animals, from Brazilian and foreign authors, members of not of SBED, which is the Brazilian chapter of the International Association for the Study of Pain (IASP), which publishes the Pain journal.

Periodicity - Trimestral

Other related data - Journal's abbreviated title is Rev. dor, which shall be used in references, footnotes and legends.
