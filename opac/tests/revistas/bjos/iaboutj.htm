Basic Information

The Brazilian Journal of Oral Sciences is an international non-profit journal, which publishes Original Research Articles, Short Communications, Literature Reviews, Case Reports, and Letters to the Editor, dealing with Dentistry or related disciplines. This Journal is published by the Piracicaba Dental School – UNICAMP. The periodicity is threemonthly (March, June, September and December) and its scope is the publication of basic and applied studies, in English.

Its abbreviated title is Braz J Oral Sci., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
