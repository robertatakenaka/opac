Basic information

Revista Brasileira de Educação Médica publishes debates, analyses and results of investigations into issues considered relevant for medical education.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. bras. educ. med., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographicalreferences and strips.
