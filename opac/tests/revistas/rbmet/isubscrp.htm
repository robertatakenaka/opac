SUBSCRIPTION

 

The RBMET is distributed without charge for all the SBMET regular associates (around 350) and for the main Brazilian University libraries and Research institutions. Currently nine foreign countries libraries receive regularly RBMET issues, being two in Europe, two in the United States and the others in Latin American. The published papers are also available online in the RBMET website (www.rbmet.org.br)

For acquisition of articles issues or for questions related to RBMET one should sent an e-mail to the Editor Chief or to the editor assistant at: rbmet@rbmet.org.br or rbmet@cptec.inpe.br

The online journal is free and open access.
