Basic Information

The Organizações & Sociedade (O&S) journal is published by Escola de Administração - Universidade Federal da Bahia - EAUFBA. The journal has existed for 18 years and boasts  remarkable academic production in Administration and connected fields. As a quarterly publication, O&S aims to provide a space to publish articles by researchers and post-graduate students dealing with subjects in the general field of organizations and society.

The journal publishes original (unpublished) texts (in Portuguese, Spanish and English) from all areas of Administration and its interfaces with other areas of Applied Social Sciences and Social Sciences. The interdisciplinary ethos of O&S can be identified in the Qualis CAPES Index where O&S was awarded A2 level in the area of Administration, Accounting and Tourism, B2 level in Political Science and International Relations, Social Service, and Interdisciplinary areas; B3 level in Psychology and Urban and Regional Planning/Demography; B4 level in Engineering III, Sociology, Education, Literature/Linguistics, and Agricultural Sciences I, and B5 level in the areas of Economics and Medicine II.

The journal has a high standard editorial committee made up of researchers from various regions of Brazil as well as from abroad to guarantee the quality of the published articles. The main objective of the O&S journal is to offer the international and national community a space for the communication and dissemination of scientific production of unpublished articles in order to expand the scope of knowledge. The journal provides open and free of charge access to all the issues.

Intellectual Property

Best practice and ethics policy in O & S publication


Articles submitted to the journal Organizações e Sociedade (O&S) for publication must be in line with the ethical guidelines set forth in the Conselho Nacional de Desenvolvimento Científico e Tecnológico (CNPq) and the Committee on Publication Ethics - (COPE).

Ethical Conduct of the Editorial Team

The O&S editorial team should:
- To be responsible for all the publications of the magazine;
- Make effort to satisfy demands of readers, authors and reviewers;
- Make constant improvements in the magazine;
- Ensure the quality of the works published in the journal;
- Safeguarding the confidentiality of the evaluators' identity;
- Ensure for the guarantee of freedom of expression, acting in an integral, impartial, transparent and agile manner;
- To check if the published works are in accordance with the ethical guidelines accepted nationally and internationally;
- Investigate in detail suspected cases of misconduct;
- Ensure ethical conduct of the entire submission process, peer review and publication in the journal.

Ethical Conduct of Evaluators

O&S reviewers should:

- Acting in an ethical, with probity and impartial manner;
- Alert the editorial staff about the occurrence of similar work already published or under analysis in another journal;
- Not to take advantage of inside information, obtained due to its condition of evaluator, for any purpose;
- Maintain the confidentiality of the material in evaluation process;
- Ensure compliance the period of analysis of the articles agreed with the editorial team;
- Communicate to the editorial team the occurrence of conflict of interest that damages the integrity of the evaluation process.

Ethical Conduct of Authors
When submitting papers to O & S, authors should:

- Ensure the integrity of the research that bases the article;
- Guarantee the authorship and originality of the work;
- To verify the correct authenticity of the citations when referring to articles and data of third parties;
- Ensure for the ethical writing and veracity of its text regarding the commercial, intellectual or industrial property of a third party;
- Ensure that your article was not submitted to another journal;
- Notify speedy the editorial team about any inaccuracies in the article
- Ensure that all authors who subscribe to the article have effectively participated in its elaboration and have mastery over its content.

Intellectual property

In order to ensure the protection of authors' intellectual rights, O&S uses the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 License in all articles it publishes, except in cases of specific copyright designation.  
O&S adopts the Plagiarism15.5.4 program to verify plagiarism and self-plagiarism in texts submitted

Policies to combat breaches of good practices in scientific production and publication

In order to eliminate ethical misconduct, O&S is guided by COPE policies (https://publicationethics.org/resources/flowcharts) in cases of suspected plagiarism, duplication of publication, changes in authorship, conflicts of interest, ethical problem, data manufacturing and misuse of articles by the evaluator, when submitting and even after the article is published.

In case of suspicion of misconduct or doubts regarding the good practices of publication in O&S, contact the e-mail revistaoes@ufba.br.
