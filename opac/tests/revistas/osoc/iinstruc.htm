Scope and Policy

Focus:

Organizações & Sociedade (O&S) focuses on publishing articles of a broad epistemological range covering mainstream articles as well as articles on the border of knowledge about non-conventional subjects. The O&S journal includes articles on  public, private and  third sector organizations addressing the following fields: human resources, theories and organizational culture, management of knowledge, environmental management, public management, strategy, development and local power, organizational learning, decision process, restructuring of labour, ethics and diversity in organizations. It means the journal is a vehicle for many movements within contemporary society.

Scope:

Organizações & Sociedade aims to publish articles by teachers, researchers and students in the general field of organizations and society studies.  The articles must be original (in Portuguese, Spanish or English) on a broad range of subjects in Administration and its interfaces with Applied Social Sciences, Social Sciences and other linked areas. The O&S journal has an editorial committee made up of researchers from the national scene as well as from abroad keeping high standards so as to guarantee the academic quality of the articles published.  The main objective of the O&S journal is to supply the academic community with articles which can give a substantial contribution to amplifying knowledge in the field of Administration.
The articles submitted to the journal go through a first evaluation on a desk review basis. On acceptance they enter the normal process of evaluation by at least two referees with skills in the area of knowledge of the article in a double blind review process.

Peer review:

Any article submitted to the O&S journal is sent to two referees, members of the Editorial Committee or ad hoc referees, who are designated according to their knowledge in order to match the subject of the article. Referees are given a month to review the article.

Articles are evaluated following the criteria of content (pertinency, advisability , clear communication, up to date and original) and form (clear writing,  use of technical norms, clear  presentation of graphs, tables, maps, etc) as well as other relevant criteria.

A key component of the evaluation is the principle of a double blind review, followed by O&S.

The author (s)
Be aware that authors or coauthors with articles accepted in desk review may only submit another work after 12 months from the date of the last submission.

Be aware that the work will be published only when authors or co-authors have not published in the same year.

Be aware that the work of authors or co-authors of the same institution and locality will be published if the number of works published in a year with authors and coauthors in these conditions does not exceed 15% of the total, in order not to set up concentration.

Be aware that work with authors or co-authors of the Editorial Board and the Federal University of Bahia will only be published if: these authors and coauthors do not participate in the evaluation process, and the number of papers published in a year with authors and co-authors under these conditions Does not exceed 15% of the total, so as not to configure endogenous.

Be aware that you should not have as author or co-author executive editorial staff members, including special issue editors.

Be aware that, upon submitting, the article must first go through desk review, and may be rejected immediately or sent to the evaluation process.

Be aware that, if the article goes through desk review, it will be designated without identification of the authors, so as to allow the "blind evaluation" to at least two evaluators with mastery over the theme content (peer review).

Be aware that when evaluated, the text can receive criticism and suggestions in several review rounds. At the end of this process, the text may still be refused or accepted for publication.

Be aware that authors must agree to the following copyright terms: (a) authors retain the copyright and grant to the journal Organizações e Sociedade (O & S) and the School of Administration of the Federal University of Bahia (EAUFBA) the right Of first publication, with work simultaneously licensed under Creative Commons Attribution License, allowing the sharing of work; (B) authors are authorized to enter into additional contracts separately, for non-exclusive distribution of the version of the work published in this journal (eg publish in institutional repository or as a book chapter), with acknowledgment of authorship and initial publication in that journal; (C) authors are allowed and encouraged to publish and distribute their work online (eg in institutional repositories or on their personal page).

There are no fees for submission and evaluation of articles.

The Journal adopts Plagiarism 15.5.4 system for plagiarism identification.

 

Form and preparation of manuscripts

Manuscripts submitted to the O&S journal can be written in the following languages: Portuguese, Spanish, English, and must follow the criteria below:

  1. Preferably original and fitting the area of interest of the journal
  2. No spaces should be left between the lines of the text, one of the most widely known text editors should be used, and the text should be no longer than 20 pages.
  3. Manuscripts must start with an Abstract of no more than 15 lines (150 words), in Portuguese and English, with 5 key words.
  4. Manuscripts which contain drawings, photos, prints should be sent in the original form in order to guarantee quality in printing
  5. Manuscripts are to be sent to revistaoes@ufba.br with authorization from the authors to publish the article if accepted.
  6. Manuscripts must be sent with information about the authors: full name and their institution, a short CV of the authors, address, telephone number and e-mail address.
  7. A maximum of  3 authors in the manuscript is accepted
  8. Once accepted by the desk review stage the manuscripts are sent to at least two referees. The authors are notified of acceptance or rejection of the submitted article. If accepted, each author is given three copies of the issue in which his/her article appears
  9. The Editorial Committee has the right to accept and to suggest modifications in the manuscripts according to the referees, or reject manuscripts submitted to the O&S journal
  10. Quotations in the text must follow the form (AUTHOR, year) and References must be listed at the end of the text following the norms of the Associação Brasileira de Normas Técnicas (ABNT NBR 10520:2002 and NBR 6023:2002). When there is a page number for a quotation, it must be added. Example: (GONÇALVES, 2000, p. 95).  Several papers by the same author in the same year must be distinguished by adding a lower-case letter following alphabetical order. Example:  (GONÇALVES, 2000a, 2000b).

References presented at the end of the text must be in alphabetical order according to the surname of the author (lower-case letter), name, title, subtitle (if applied), edition, place, editor, date of publication, Page and/or volume, according to the ABNT norms (ABNT NBR 6023:2002). Before submitting, the author must check the citations with the references presented.  See  some examples below.

The following Submisson Rules should also be heeded:

Submission Rules (obligatory):

The submitted article must be original and not have been evaluated for publication by any other journal; if this is not the case, the author must justify in "Comments to the Editor".

Files for submission must come in Microsoft Word, RTF or similar.

All addresses "URL" in the text (eg: http:pkp.ubc.ca) are working.

Single spacing must be used and it must be written in a 12 point font; italics instead of underline should be used; figures and tables can be inserted throughout the text, and not at the end.

The text should follow the requirements of the format established by the journal according to the Instructions (Guidelines) for authors, as defined above. In the sections of the journal reviewed by peers, the author's identity is removed, and the "author" and "year" are used in the bibliography and footnotes, instead of authors' names, titles, etc. The author's name is removed in "Document Properties" (Propriedades do documento), option in the menu "File" of MS Word.

Full Responsibility of Authors: the opinions expressed by the authors are the full responsibility of the respective authors.

 

Sending of manuscripts

Submissions must be performed only via our website www.revistaoes.ufba.br and ought to follow the instructions for authors.
