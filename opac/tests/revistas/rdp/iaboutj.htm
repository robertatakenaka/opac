Basic Information

Direito e Práxis Journal is an academic, online journal, created in 2010 and published by the State University of Rio de Janeiro Graduate Law Program. The journal publishes quarterly academic papers on Theory and Philosophy of Law, Sociology of Law and Political Philosophy, and also interdisciplinary research based on critical methodologies and approaches. Papers can be submitted in Portuguese, English and Spanish.

Título abreviado: Rev. Direito e Práx.
