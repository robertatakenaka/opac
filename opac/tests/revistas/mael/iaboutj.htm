Basic Information

Machado de Assis em linha: revista eletrônica de estudos machadianos (http://machadodeassis.fflch.usp.br) is an academic journal devoted to the work of Machado de Assis, edited by Hélio de Seixas Guimarães and Marta de Senna and. Its main goal is to promote and disseminate knowledge about the author's work in its multiple aspects. The Journal’s commitment is to academic excellence.

The journal's main areas of interest are literary studies, Brazilian literature and comparative literature, provided they deal with the work of Machado de Assis.

Its abbreviated title is Machado de Assis Linha, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

The journal is published every four months, in April, August, and December.
