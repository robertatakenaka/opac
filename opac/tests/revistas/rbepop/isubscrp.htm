SUBSCRIPTION

 

The journal is distributed without extra charge to members of ABEP and to several research institutions. It can also be acquired through annual subscriptions or individual purchase upon request at rebep@abep.org.br The online version of Revista Brasileira de Estudos de População is prepared using methodology developed by FAPESP/BIREME Project for Electronic Periodicals. This version, which includes full texts of articles, is currently available free of charge.

Archive issues from its installation is are available free-of-charge in full text at Rebep’s Web Page www.rebep.org.br

The online journal is free and open access.
