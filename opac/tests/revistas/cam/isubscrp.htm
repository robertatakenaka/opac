SUBSCRIPTION

 

Computational & Applied Mathematics is published three times a year under the auspices of the Sociedade Brasileira de Matemática Aplicada e Computacional.

Annual subscription rate
The journal subscription rate is US$ 170.00 plus US$ 10.00 for mail and handling charges. The single issue rate is US$ 75.00 including mail and handkling charges. All orders must be accompanied by payment and should be sent to the journal address for correspondence.

Computational & Applied Mathematics is freely distributed to members of SBMAC. So, membership to SBMAC is an automatic procedure for subscription.

All subscriptions and changes of address should be sent to the address below.
