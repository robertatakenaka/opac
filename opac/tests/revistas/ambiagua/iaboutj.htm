Basic Information

Ambiente & Água - An Interdisciplinary Journal of Applied Science (AMBIAGUA) is a journal of the University of Taubaté, edited by the Institute for Environmental Research in Hydrographic Basins (IPABHi), that publishes original articles which contribute to the advancement of Environmental Sciences and Water Resources. The journal began in 2006 and published an edition every four months until December 31st, 2013). Beginning on January, 2017,all submissions must be in English and an issue will be published every other month. Starting on January 2018, the journal adopted a continuous publication model divided in six numbers per year..

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Ambient. Água, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references.
