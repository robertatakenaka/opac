Basic Information

The Revista Brasileira de Saúde e Produção Animal is an electronic journal for the publication of scientific papers and review within the animal health and production and Fisheries Resources. It works only with processing and publishing online making it more agile.

The RBSPA publishes four issues per year: March, June, September and December. The number of articles per issue is 25, totaling 100/year.

The abbreviation of its title is Rev. bras. saúde prod. anim. that should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
