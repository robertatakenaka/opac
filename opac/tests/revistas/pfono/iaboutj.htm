Basic information

Pró-Fono Revista de Atualização Científica (ISSN 0104-5687 – printed version; ISSN 1679-3919 – digital version - CD) was funded by two speech-language pathologists, Heliane Campanatti-Ostiz and Maria Valéria Schmidt Goffi Gómez, in 1989.  The Journal was published semiannually until 2001, every four months till 2006 and since then it has been published every three months.

Aim: to preferably publish original research articles that significantly contribute to the development of knowledge within the field of the Speech, Language and Hearing Science. Other categories accepted for publication are: Reviews and Systematic Reviews, Case Reports, Book Reviews, Commentaries, Letters to the Editor, Research Letters, Clinical Reminders, Fillers/Special Sections, Conference Reports, Websites.

Its abbreviated title is Pró-Fono R. Atual. Cient., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
