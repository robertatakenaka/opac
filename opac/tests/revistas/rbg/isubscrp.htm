SUBSCRIPTION

 

The annual subscription gives you the right to receive 4 (four) issues of the Revista Brasileira de Geofísica (RBGf - Brazilian Journal of Geophysics) and sometimes a special issue.

The members of the Sociedade Brasileira de Geofísica (SBGf – Brazilian Geophysical Society) who pay the annual fee receive the Revista Brasileira de Geofísica free of charges.

The price for subscribing one volume of the printed version of the Revista Brasileira de Geofísica is:

Individual:

  • R$ 75,00 for Brazil
  • US$ 40.00 for all other countries

Institutional:

  • R$ 75,00 for Brazil
  • US$ 40.00 for all other countries

The journal is also distributed by means of exchange or donation to Academic Geophysical Institutions.

Payment can be made by check in U$ dollars - in the name of Sociedade Brasileira de Geofísica, or by postal money orders in U$ dollars.

Orders should be accompanied by complete address, telephone and fax numbers, and e-mail.

The SciELO online version of the Revista Brasileira de Geofísica is based on the methodology developed by the FAPESP/BIREME Project on Electronic Periodicals. Presently, all published material is available online free of charge.
