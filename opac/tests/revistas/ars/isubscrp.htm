SUBSCRIPTION

 

The journal is complimentary distributed and/or given in exchange partly to schools and universities of art and partly to a selected group, non-university art institutes (such as museums and cultural centers), professors, artists, critics and theoreticians who work in the art field both in Brazil and abroad.

In accordance to the principle that providing free access to scientific production leads to a broader and more democratic circulation of knowledge, ARS offers unlimited direct access to its content through the following websites:

- www.scielo.br/ars

- http://www.revistas.usp.br/ars
