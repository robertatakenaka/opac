Manuscript Checklist

 

1. Send the original manuscript and 4 printed copies to the Editor.

2. Include name and address of the corresponding author, with e-mail and fax..

3. Include the title of the manuscript in English and Portuguese, up to 84 characters.

4. Check that the text, including abstract, tables and references, is in Arial type and double-spaced with 3-cm margins.

5. Include the abbreviated title with 40 characters, as the running title.

6. Include, in separate pages, abstracts structured as original

research articles in English, Portuguese and Spanish (for manuscripts in this language).

7. Include, in separate pages, original abstracts of non-research manuscripts in English, Portuguese and Spanish (for manuscripts in this language).

8. Include the forms, signed by each one of the authors, of "authorship responsibility" and "copyright transfer".

9. Determine the level of participation of each author when there are more than six authors.

10. Include financing agencies and process number.

11. Acknowledge, when appropriate, that the article is based on a thesis dissertation, providing the name of the institution and year of defense.

12. Make sure all references are Vancouver-style alphabetically numbered and they are all referred in the text. Provide a separate reference list.

13. Include permission from the editors for the reproduction of published figure or tables.
