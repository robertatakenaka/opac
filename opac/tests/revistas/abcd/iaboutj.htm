Basic information

ABCD - ARQUIVOS BRASILEIROS DE CIRURGIA DIGESTIVA, the official publication of the Colégio Brasileiro de Cirurgia Digestiva – CBCD (Brazilian College of Digestive Surgery), is a quarterly Journal, published in a single volume per year, whose mission is  the publication of articles of clinical and experimental studies that foster the advancement of research, teaching and assistance in surgical, clinical, and endoscopic gastroenterology, and related areas. Its major sections are original articles, review or update articles, case reports, editorials (solicited) and letters to the Editor. Other sections may be included depending on the interest of the Journal or the need to  publish relevant subject matters not suitable for the categories above. 

Its abbreviated title is Arq. Bras. Cir. Dig., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
