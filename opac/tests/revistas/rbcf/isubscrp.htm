SUBSCRIPTION

 

The signature is done through the sending of requests to Institutions and persons with interest.

The price is determined annually by the Editorial Board.

The payment must be done through the bank deposit or by the sending a nominal check to Faculdade de Ciências Farmacêuticas da Universidade de São Paulo.

The donation basis is only to public organizations of teaching and research.

The exchange can be done with those journals of interest to the Library of Conjunto das Químicas da Universidade de São Paulo.
