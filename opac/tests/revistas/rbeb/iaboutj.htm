Basic Information

The Brazilian Journal of Biomedical Engineering (ISSN 1517-3151 / 1984-7742) is published quarterly and is dedicated to publishing research in all fields of Biomedical Engineering. In addition, this journal serves as a forum for the establishment of developing policies and incorporation of health technologies by the public or private sectors.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Bras. Eng. Bioméd., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
