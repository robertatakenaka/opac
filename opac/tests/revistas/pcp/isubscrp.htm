SUBSCRIPTION

 

The journal Psychology: Science and Profession is open access to the public, according with its mission and policy that seeks the diffusion of scientific knowledge and advances in different areas of psychology. Public access is essential to spread knowledge and permit consultation by the community, encouraging the development of socially relevant scientific research and improving the quality of academic and professional activities.

The online journal is free and open access.
