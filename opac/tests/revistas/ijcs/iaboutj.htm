Basic Information

The International Journal of Cardiovascular Sciences (Int. J. Cardiovasc. Sci.) is published by the Sociedade Brasileira de Cardiologia (Brazilian Society of Cardiology), and is issued on a bimonthly basis. Launched in 2015, this journal follows the previous ones, Revista da SOCERJ and Revista Brasileira de Cardiologia, created in 1998 and 2010, respectively. It is available in hard copy and online, in PDF at: http://www.onlineijcs.org The online version presents the manuscripts in English and in Portuguese.

Its abbreviated title is Int. J. Cardiovasc. Sci., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
