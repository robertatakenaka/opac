Basic Information

Revista Caatinga (ISSN 0100-316X printed and 1983-2125 online) is a journal published by the Dean of Research and Post-graduation program of the Universidade Federal Rural do Semi-Árido (UFERSA) since 1976. At present, the magazine is a quarterly publication with the average number of 30 academic papers per issue.

Its aim is provide and release freely high level publications to the scientific community about relevant researches and results in the fields of Agrarian sciences and Natural resources. RC is the short title to the Revista Caatinga.
