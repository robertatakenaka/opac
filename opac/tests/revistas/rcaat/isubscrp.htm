Revista Caatinga has a printed version, however there is not marketing. The journals are printed by the Universidade Federal Rural do Semi-Árido and sent freely to institutions with some link with the university without the need to request. The copies are sent by mail.

To the Revista Caatinga, a minimum amount of copies are sent to be stored in its printed files and to be available for readers, authors and those who it may concern. Revista Caatinga does not charge to access the papers published.
