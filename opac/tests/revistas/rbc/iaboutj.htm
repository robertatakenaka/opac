Basic information

Revista Brasilera de Coloproctologia to publish original contributions in Colo-Proctology, Surgery y Gastroenterology.

The goal of the journal is to presentation of works on human medicine and surgery, elaborated by national and foreign specialists.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. bras. colo-proctol., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
