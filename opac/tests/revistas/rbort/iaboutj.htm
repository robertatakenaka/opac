Basic information

Revista Brasileira de Ortopedia (Rev Bras Ortop.) - ISSN 0102-3616 is the scientific publication body of the Brazilian Society of Orthoapedics and Traumatology intended to disseminate articles contributing to the improvement and development of orthopaedic practice, research and teaching and similar specialties.

Published on a bimonthly basis, and headquartered at the Brazilian Society of Orthopaedics - Alameda Lorena, 427 - 2º andar - Jd Paulista, SP, Brazil.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. bras. ortop., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
