Instructions for authors and editorial policy

JCBS is a formal publication of the Brazilian Computer Society - Sociedade Brasileira de Computação. Its aim is to publish original quality research papers, serving as an environment for stimulating and disseminating research in all fields of computer science. Both theoretical and experimental papers are welcome. Contributions will be considered for publication in JCBS if they have not to been published previously and are not under consideration for publication elsewhere.

Acceptance of papers for publication is subject to a peer review procedure and is conditional on revisions being made given comments from referees. Only papers digitally submitted in the format specified at http://www.sbc.org.br will be reviewed.

Authors whose papers are accepted will be requested to transfer the copyright rights to the Brazilian Computer Society, to ensure the widest possible dissemination of the paper. It is the sole responsibility of the authors to obtain the necessary permissions to cite other sources. The authors are fully responsible for the correctness of the information contained in the paper.

Please submit a digital copy of your paper as an attachment of a message to jbcs@sbc.org.br.
