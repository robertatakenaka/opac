Basic Information

The Archives of Clinical Psychiatry is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published 6 times a year.

Its abbreviated title is Arch. Clin. Psychiatry (São Paulo)., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
