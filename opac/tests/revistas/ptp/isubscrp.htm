SUBSCRIPTION

 

Psicologia: Teoria e Pesquisa is a journal published every three months by Instituto de Psicologia da Universidade de Brasília [Institute of Psychology - University of Brasília].

Subscription of the printed version of the journal should be directly requested to:

Revista Psicologia: Teoria e Pesquisa
Secretaria de Divulgação
Universidade de Brasília - Instituto de Psicologia
70910-900 Brasília DF.

SPECIAL OFFER

Psicologia: Teoria e Pesquisa offers a special discount of 20% for personal subscription of undergraduates and graduate students for orders of at least 10 subscriptions. Request for this special discount should be sent together with evidence by the respective college, or a letter signed by a lecturer certifying the condition of the students.

The online journal is free and open access.
