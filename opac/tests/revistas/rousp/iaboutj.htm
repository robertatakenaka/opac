Basic information

The Revista de Odontologia da Universidade de São Paulo is published four times a year in January, April, July and October by the Faculdade de Odontologia da Universidade de São Paulo, with the objective of publishing works related to basic and applied research, as well as articles intended to disseminate and update subjects in the field of dentistry.

Published since 1987, the abbreviated title of the journal is Rev. Odontol. Univ. São Paulo, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, bibliographical references and legends.
