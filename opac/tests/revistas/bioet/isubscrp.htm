The Revista Bioética is distributed free of charge to interested parties requesting, and federal institutions of specialty health care, regional medical councils, medical associations, research groups in ethics, bioethics and human rights, registered in the CNPq, stricto and lato sensu courses in bioethics, and the libraries of all Brazilians universities.

The online journal is free and open access.
