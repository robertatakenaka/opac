SUBSCRIPTION

 

There are two ways for the interested party to have access to RBCE's print editions: 1) be a member of the Colégio Brasileiro de Ciências do Esporte (Brazilian College for Sports Sciences); for such, check: http://www.cbce.org.br/br/socios/; 2) subscribe directly from Editora Autores Associados (old publisher responsible for the publication of RBCE), through its site (http://www.autoresassociados.com.br/), or from Editora Tribo da Ilha (current publisher responsible for the publication of RBCE): http://editoratribo.blogspot.com/. Interested parties can also obtain separate RBCE numbers at the main bookstores in the country.

At those two addresses, the interested party shall have all the information that refers to forms of request, prices, forms of payment, etc.

The online journal is free and open access.
