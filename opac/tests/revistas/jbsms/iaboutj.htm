Basic information

The Journal of the Brazilian Society of Mechanical Sciences is edited and published quarterly by the Brazilian Society of Mechanical Sciences (ABCM).

Its abbreviated title is J. Braz. Soc. Mech. Sci., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

 

 

Information services

  • Applied Mechanics Reviews

  • Engineering Information, Inc.
