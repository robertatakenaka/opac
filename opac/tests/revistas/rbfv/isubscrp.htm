SUBSCRIPTION

 

Printed version of the Brazilian Journal of Plant Physiology is distributed to the members of the Brazilian Society of Plant Physiology.

Brazil:
Annual subscription: R$ 75.00

Together with the subscription form (http://www.cpa.unicamp.br/bjpp), send a check in name of the "Sociedade Brasileira de Fisiologia Vegetal" to the Treasurer of the society. Subscribers of BJPP automatically become members of the Brazilian Society of Plant Physiology.

Other countries:
Annual subscription: US$ 75.00

Mail the subscription form (http://www.cpa.unicamp.br/bjpp) to the Treasurer of the Brazilian Society of Plant Physiology and send a money order to the bank:

Bank: Banco do Brasil
