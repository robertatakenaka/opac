Basic Information

The Revista Brasileira de Fisiologia Vegetal (Brazilian Journal of Plant Physiology) is the official journal of the Brazilian Society of Plant Physiology and is devoted to publish original research contributions in various fields of plant physiology.

Revista Brasileira de Fisiologia Vegetal publishes regular papers, short communications, minireviews and Brazilian minireviews.

To briefly refer to the journal, the short title Rev. Bras. Fisiol. Veg. is recommended, which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
