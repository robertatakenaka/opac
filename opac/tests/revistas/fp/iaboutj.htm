Basic Information

Fisioterapia e Pesquisa (ISSN 1809-2950), which is a continuation of the journal of Physical Therapy of USP (ISSN 1413-7879), is a scientific journal with the goal of disseminating the scientific production of Physical Therapy and other areas of health since 1994. This journal has a spread in the area of physiotherapy that is the emphasis on publication of scientific articles focused on clinical research and has greatly contributed to the updating and consolidation of Evidence-Based Physiotherapy.

Periodicity: quarterly.

Its abbreviated title is Fisioter. Pesqui., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
