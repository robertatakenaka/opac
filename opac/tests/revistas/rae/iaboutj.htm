Basic information

RAE-Revista de Administração de Empresas is published by FGV´s Sao Paulo School of Business Administration (EAESP) and itis interested in publishing papers that are theoretical in their development, empirical works and essays.

Since January 2011 RAE-eletrônica became integrated with the RAE-Revista de Administração de Empresas. Accordingly, the publication of the RAE changed from quarterly to bimonthly (6 issues/year).

The theoretically developed papers must be backed up by extensive bibliographic research and must propose new models and interpretations for phenomena that are relevant to the field of business administration.

Empirical studies must advance knowledge in the area, through research that is methodologically well founded, that has been conducted in a prudent manner and appropriately analyzed.

Essays are scientific contributions that are freer in form. These papers must have a critical and creative approach, revealing new perspectives that encourage the journals readers to reflect upon themes that are relevant to the particular area of knowledge.
We accept contributions from both Brazil and abroad in the fields of business administration and related areas. We encourage a wide range of approaches and views.

It is a sine qua non condition that this will be the first time the paper is to be published. All papers that are submitted are assessed using the double blind review system.

It is classified by the CAPES Qualis system as a "Brazilian A" journal.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. adm. empres., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
