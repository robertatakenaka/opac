Basic Information

The Revista Brasileira de Hematologia e Hemoterapia is a three-monthly scientific publication of the Associação Brasileira de Hematologia e Hemoterapia (Brasilian Association of Hematology and Hemotherapy)

The abbreviation of its title, Rev Bras Hematol Hemoter., should be used as footnotes or in the references etc. when citing articles published in the journal.

 

Indexing

Articles published in the Revista Brasileira de Hematologia e Hemoterapia are indexed in or summarized by-nc-nd:

  • LILACS
  • EXTRAMED
  • Scopus
  • Pubmed/PMC
  • Capes/Qualis
