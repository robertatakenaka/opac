SUBSCRIPTION

 

The Revista Brasileira de Hematologia e Hemoterapia is a two-monthly scientific publication. The prices of subscription for the print version are:

In Brazil

Annual subscription – R$ 150.00
Single copy – R$ 20.00

Overseas

Annual subscription – US$ 150.00
Single copy – US$ 15.00

The Revista Brasileira de Hematologia e Hemoterapia is distributed for free to regional medical libraries and medical, pharmaceutical, and biochemistry schools and societies with similar interests in South, Central and North America.
