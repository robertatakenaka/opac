SUBSCRIPTION

 

The Revista Brasileira de Farmacognosia/Brazilian Journal of Pharmacognosy is a quarterly publication of the Sociedade Brasileira de Farmacognosia/Brazilian Society of Pharmacognosy freely distributed among all members.
The annual subscription rates are effective as of January 1st of the subscription year. Institutional rates apply to libraries, hospitals, research institutes, and corporations. Only qualified professionals and students are eligible for individual subscriptions. All journal subscriptions are on a non-returnable calendar year basis. Multiple year subscriptions are not available. Delivery of subscription will start upon receipt of payment.

  • Individual Subscription (Physical Person)
    Brazil: R$ 160,00/Foreign countries: US$ 160.00
  • Student
    Brazil: R$ 110,00/Foreign countries: US$ 110.00
  • Institutional (Juridical Person)
    Brazil: R$ 1.000,00/Foreign countries: US$ 1000.00
  • Single issue (under consultation) Brazil: R$ 35,00/Foreign countries: US$ 35,00
