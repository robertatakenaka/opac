Basic Information

Revista Latinoamericana de Psicopatologia Fundamental is the official organ of the University Association for Research in Fundamental Psychopathology. The association is a scientific society that brings together professors at public and private universities around the world. The journal is dedicated to publishing original editorials, articles and book reviews in psychopathology that take subjectivity into account. It also publishes little-known texts that nonetheless constitute important and useful historical sources. Articles and essays resulting from research using the clinical method based on case studies with questions yet to be investigated are especially important for the journal. The journal's first issue came out in March of 1998, and is now published quarterly, with issues in March, June, September and December of each year, containing articles in the Portuguese, Spanish, French and English languages. All articles contain five abstracts with the same contents, in Portuguese, Spanish, French, German and English. Each issue also publishes brief notes on the background of each author, with their respective addresses for contact.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. latinoam. psicopatol. fundam., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
