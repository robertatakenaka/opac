Basic Information

The journal Revista Brasileira de Ciência Avícola (Brazilian Journal of Poultry Science) is a peer-review journal published with the coordination of the Publishing Committee of the APINCO Foundation of Poultry Science and Technology.

Its abbreviated title is Rev. Bras. Cienc. Avic., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
