Deposits must be made in the Banco Itaú (bank n. 341; agency 1538, account number 17189-1), in behalf of Fundação da Universidade Federal do Paraná (Funpar). The deposit receipt must be sent to the journal accompanied by the subscriber's name and full address (including zip code, city, state, telephone, fax, e-mail etc.). A payment receipt will be sent by mail, and the issues just after their release.
