Basic Informations

Brazilian Journal of Oceanography is a journal of the Instituto Oceanográfico da Universidade de São Paulo aiming at the publication of scientific contributions on oceanography including physical oceanography, marine chemistry, marine geology and geophysics, biological oceanography, fisheries and related fields.

Its abbreviated title is Braz. j. oceanogr., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.
