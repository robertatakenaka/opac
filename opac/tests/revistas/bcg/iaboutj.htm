Basic Information

The Bulletin of Geodetic Sciences (BCG) (ISSN: 1982-2170) is a quarterly journal which publishes (in English) original papers in the areas of Geodetic Sciences and correlated ones.

The BCG has an Editorial Board which is helped for an external Advisory Board (from researches from different institutions and countries) highly qualified in the fields of Geodesy and Surveying, Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing, Cartography and Geographic Information Systems. The editors can accept or reject the submitted papers according its field.

The volumes published since 1997 are available through the electronic service of the Federal University of Parana (http://revistas.ufpr.br). The same content is also available at Scielo's website (www.scielo.org).

Citations should follow the example:

NARVAES, I. S; dos SANTOS, J. R; da SILVA, A. Q. Analysis of structural parameters of forest typologies USING L-band SAR data. Bulletin of Geodetic Sciences [online]. 2010, vol.16, n.3, pp.475-489. ISSN 1982-2170. http://dx.doi.org/10.1590/S1982-21702010000300007.
