Basic Information

The Journal of Applied Oral Science is a continuation of the Revista da Faculdade de Odontologia de Bauru.

The Journal of Applied Oral Science operates under a continuous publication online modality. This model results in annual volumes, without issue numbers.

The Revista da Faculdade de Odontologia de Bauru was published between 1993-2001 and continued from 2002 as the Journal of Applied Oral Science, volume 11, # 1, and kept the numbering sequence of the former.

The main goal of the Journal of Applied Oral Science is to publish results from original research as well as invited case reports and invited reviews in the fields of dentistry and speech-language pathology and audiology and related sciences areas.

Its abbreviated title is J. Appl. Oral Sci., and it should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and bibliographic strips.
